Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 box yellow cake mix
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cake mix and melted butter until well combined. Press the mixture evenly into the prepared pan to form the crust.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the shredded coconut and chopped pecans. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the crust in the pan.
  4. Pour the sweetened condensed milk evenly over the coconut and pecan layer.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  6. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. Once cooled, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the bars out of the pan. Cut into squares and serve.
